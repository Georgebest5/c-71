Review on the movies

Avengers Endgame
Excellent film to culminate MCU ride

I loved the movie a lot As I am a great fan of marvel Avengers: Endgame', which marks the end of the Infinity Saga, is spellbinding and surely an enthralling experience. The last film of the 'Avengers' franchise is remarkable and doesn't disappoint. Watching all our favorite superheroes in one film is just surpassing. Marvel has been working on this grand culmination ever since they released 'Iron Man'. I'm damn sure that all of their hard work and ambition has paid off.


The Lion King
Excellent film to culminate MCU ride

Out of all the movies that had ever come out of Disney, "The Lion King" is what I consider to be their greatest achievement of all time. Right as the opening sequence commenced with the "Circle of Life", my heart was so pumped, down to the point where I felt like a kid again every time I saw it. The story was epic on giving an insight of the steps in becoming the next-in-line, the challenges on losing someone dear to them and overcoming their fears, and fighting for what's rightfully theirs.
